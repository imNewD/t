Explore Northern Albania

Explore the breathtaking beauty of Northern Albania, where vibrant cities like Tirana and Shkodra coexist with picturesque mountain villages such as Theth and Valbona. Tirana, the capital, is a vibrant city with a mix of Ottoman, Italian, and communist-era architecture. Shkodra, located near Lake Shkodra, is known for its medieval history and stunning landscapes.

Theth and Valbona, nestled in the Albanian Alps, offer a tranquil retreat with pristine nature, traditional stone houses, and warm hospitality. Visit Theth to explore the Theth National Park and the iconic Blue Eye waterfall. In Valbona, embark on a hike through Valbona Valley, surrounded by high peaks and dense forests.

Important Dates:
- Tirana Foundation Day: November 17
- Shkodra Carnival: March 5

Discover Middle Albania

Discover the rich history of Middle Albania, where cities like Durres, Kruja, and Berat showcase the country's cultural heritage. Durres, situated on the Adriatic Sea, is home to ancient ruins, including the Roman amphitheater and Venetian Tower. Kruja, with its historic castle, played a crucial role in Albanian history and is known for its medieval architecture.

Berat, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is often referred to as the "City of a Thousand Windows" due to its well-preserved Ottoman architecture. Explore the medieval castle, Onufri Museum, and the charming streets of Mangalem and Gorica. Middle Albania offers a journey through time, blending the past with the present.

Important Dates:
- Durres Day: October 29
- Kruja Carnival: May 20
- Berat Day: October 10

Experience South Albania

Experience the coastal charm and archaeological wonders of South Albania. Cities like Vlora, Saranda, and Gjirokastra offer a perfect blend of history and natural beauty. Vlora, overlooking the Adriatic Sea, is known for its Independence Day celebrations and beautiful beaches.

Saranda, located on the Ionian Sea, is a gateway to the ancient ruins of Butrint, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Gjirokastra, with its well-preserved Ottoman architecture, invites you to explore its historic sites, including Gjirokastra Castle and the Old Bazaar.

Whether you relax on the beaches of Ksamil, explore the ancient city of Butrint, or wander through the cobblestone streets of Gjirokastra, South Albania offers a diverse and captivating experience.

Important Dates:
- Vlora Independence Day: November 28
- Saranda Summer Festival: July 15
- Gjirokastra Old Bazaar Day: September 10
